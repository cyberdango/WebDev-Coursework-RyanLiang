In Unit 6, I learned how important feedback and iteration are in web design. I asked users and my client for opinions about my photography website and used their suggestions to improve the design. I added styled links, image borders, a light pink background, and better spacing to make the page look cleaner and more professional. I also learned how links can improve navigation and how small design details can make a website more visually appealing. This unit helped me understand that web design is a process of testing, improving, and making changes based on feedback from users and clients.
